Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Files and conversions

All file conversions performed on FreeFileConverter.org happen entirely within your browser. Files you select or drop into the converter are processed using browser APIs (Canvas API, File API, JavaScript) and are never transmitted to any server. We have no technical capability to access, store, or view your files.

Data we collect

We collect minimal data to keep the service running:

  • Anonymous page view analytics via Cloudflare Web Analytics (no cookies, no cross-site tracking)
  • Ad impressions from third-party advertising partners

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

Cookies and local storage

We do not set cookies. If you choose to add an API key using the "Unlock more formats" feature, that key is stored in your browser's local storage only — it remains on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Third-party services

This site uses third-party advertising partners, including display advertising and Infolinks. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. We do not sell or upload your converted files.

Cloudflare provides hosting and CDN services and processes network-level traffic data per their own privacy policy.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
